# ApplyWise AI

ApplyWise AI helps users manage and improve their job applications. Users can save a resume and a job posting, then analyze their compatibility using AI.

The application can:

- Identify skills that match the job posting and skills that are missing.
- Find supporting evidence in the user's resume.
- Recommend ways to prepare for the role.
- Generate grounded cover-letter drafts.
- Track job applications.

All AI-generated analysis and writing must remain grounded in the information supplied by the user. The AI must never invent qualifications, skills, achievements, or experience.
